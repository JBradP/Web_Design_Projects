Lil' Bits of Joy

bakery and Deli

Come in and enjoy a fresh brewed cup of coffee while you indulge on one of our sweet treats, or stop by on your way to work!


Coffe Menu:

  • Espresso - A shot of strong, concentrated coffee.
  • Americano - Espresso shots diluted with hot water.
  • Latte - Espresso with steamed milk and a small amount of foam.
  • Cappuccino - Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and foam.
  • Mocha - Espresso with chocolate syrup and steamed milk, topped with whipped cream.
  • Macchiato - Espresso "stained" with a dash of steamed milk.
  • Flat White - Espresso with velvety microfoam milk.
  • Cold Brew - Cold-brewed coffee served over ice.
  • Iced Latte - Espresso with cold milk and ice.
  • Frappuccino - Blended coffee drink with milk, ice, and flavorings (e.g., vanilla, caramel).
  • Pumpkin Spice Latte (seasonal) - Espresso with pumpkin spice syrup and steamed milk.
  • Peppermint Mocha (seasonal) - Espresso with peppermint syrup, chocolate, and steamed milk.
  • Matcha Latte - Matcha green tea with steamed milk.
  • Golden Milk Latte - Turmeric-infused milk with spices like cinnamon and ginger.
